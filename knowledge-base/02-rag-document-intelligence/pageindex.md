# PageIndex

PageIndex is a reasoning-based document intelligence and RAG platform for long, complex documents.

## Why it stands out
- It focuses on document understanding with traceable, verifiable answers.
- It uses a tree-structured index instead of the usual chunking-and-embedding workflow.
- The current docs emphasize that it can work without a vector database and without chunking.
- It exposes both developer-facing API and MCP-style integration paths.

## Good use cases
- Annual reports
- Legal or compliance documents
- Research papers
- Any long PDF where you want citations and explainability

## Notes for your knowledge base
- Keep PageIndex here when you want document intelligence notes that are more structured than classic RAG.
- Pair it with your RAG notes whenever you compare vector search, tree indexing, and agentic retrieval.
