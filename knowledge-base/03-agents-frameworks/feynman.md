# Feynman

Feynman is an open-source AI research agent focused on cited, source-grounded research workflows.

## What it does
- Reads papers.
- Searches the web.
- Writes drafts.
- Runs experiments.
- Cites every claim.

## Built-in workflows
- Deep research
- Literature review
- Review and critique
- Paper audit
- Replication planning
- Comparison and synthesis

## Why it belongs in this knowledge base
- It sits at the intersection of agents, research, and verification.
- It is a useful reference if you want to study how modern research agents are packaged and exposed to users.
